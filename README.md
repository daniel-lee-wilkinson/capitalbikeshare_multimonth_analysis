## Seasonality and Trends
how does total ridership vary accross months and seasons?
do certain stations show more seasonal variation than others?
is trip duration longer in certain seasons?
did average durations spike during COVID (2020-2021)? - yes! lockdowns. Durations returned to pre-COVID durations in 2022 and have declined compared to pre-COVID times.

## Weekday and weekend behaviour
are weekday and weekend usage patterns consistent month-to-month?
do weekend trips tend to be longer or start from different areas?
has the weekday pattern weakened or shifted accross the year?

## Station performance
Which stations had the highest total usage over the year?
Which stations were consistently low-traffic each month — possible candidates for removal or relocation?
Are there stations that were net sinks most months? What about net sources? (requires destination coorindate or ZIP-level aggregation)
How many stations were only active for part of the year? (seasonal openings/closures?)

# Operational timing
At what times of day are bikes most often checked out, on average, per month?
Are morning and evening rush hour peaks stable throughout the year?
How does the time between rides (station idle time) vary across months or stations?

# Data quality
Are NULL or missing values (e.g. station names, coordinates) consistent or improving over time?
Is there an increase/decrease in the number of unnamed clusters or trips with invalid durations?

## Geographic patterns (needs spatial data)
Which ZIP codes or neighbourhoods had the fastest growth in trips?
Are there areas that improved significantly in station usage or balance over the year?

## Forecasting and planning
Based on the past 12 months, can we predict next month's top 10 stations?
Which stations may need more docks if growth continues at this rate?
What areas could benefit from new stations to relieve consistent sink zones? (needs gospatial clustering or ZIPs)
